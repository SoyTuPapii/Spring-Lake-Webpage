Welcome to Spring Lake Farms LLC Nestled in the heart of Stanton, Michigan, Spring Lake Farms is a fourth generation dairy farm dedicated to providing the highest quality products, fostering sustainable farming practices, and connecting with our community. Our farm is home to happy, healthy cows, fresh local produce, and a team that is passionate about agriculture and family traditions.
