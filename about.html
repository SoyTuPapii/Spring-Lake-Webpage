A Brief History

Founded in 1920 by Allen Hardy, our farm has grown from less than 10 cows to nearly 500 today. Now spanning an impressive 800 acres, Spring Lake Farms is proudly managed by the fourth generation of the Hardy family, Bill and Jason Hardy. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, we milk 180 cows twice a day, producing an astounding 2,000 gallons of milk daily. Our milk is carefully collected and sold to local dairies, where it is transformed into delicious cheeses that our community enjoys.

Throughout the years, we have embraced modern farming practices while honoring our family traditions, ensuring that our farm not only thrives but also contributes positively to the environment and our local economy. As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to nurturing our land and livestock, fostering strong relationships within our community, and providing the freshest, highest-quality dairy products possible.
